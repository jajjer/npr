LIVE UPDATES: MAN'S APPLICATION

Man Really Wants Junior Front End Developer Job at NPR

A young developer, Jacob Jensen, has gone to great lengths to try to persuade NPR to hire him. "I really can't express how much I love NPR and how much I want this job", said Jensen. Will he get the job? Listen live to find out.

  • Latest updates on his software engineering career.

  • Why he should work for NPR.

TECHNOLOGY

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Python

I have proven expereince with HTML & CSS, particularly grid, media queries, and animations. I also have expertise with JavaScript and React, as well as familiarity with Python for backend engineering.

EDUCATION

B.S. in Psychology & a Front End Developer Certification

I graduated from the University of Minnesota with a bachelor of science in Psychology. I am currently finishing a Front End Developer certification through Practicum by Yandex.

JOBS

Looking For Front End Job

My professional background is in mental health and video production, but I am hoping to make Front End Development my new career.

ALL THINGS CONSIDERED

You Should Really Hire Me

Although I am fairly new to web development and don't necessarily have the experience that you may be looking for, I promise I will work as hard as it takes and learn what I need to know to write great code for NPR. I love what NPR stands for and it would be an honor to work for you.
